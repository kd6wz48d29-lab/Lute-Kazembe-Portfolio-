Biography

Visionary. Results-oriented.
Mission-driven.

As a strategic communications and advocacy executive with over 15 years of global leadership experience, I have shaped narratives, led high-performing multicultural teams, and driven policy and brand transformation across Africa, the Middle East, Asia, and South America.

At Oxfam International, I served as Deputy Director of Advocacy, Campaigns & Engagement — unifying 30+ global affiliates under a shared communications and engagement strategy, and strengthening global crisis and reputational risk systems that improved decision-making efficiency by 50%.

Previously, as Regional Communications Manager for the Middle East at Medair, I directed a regional communications portfolio spanning eight countries, producing award-winning humanitarian campaigns and cultivating relationships with UN agencies, foundations, and bilateral donors — stewarding a $20M portfolio.

I thrive at the intersection of strategy, influence, and impact — bridging global narratives with local realities to strengthen trust, engagement, and sustainable outcomes.
